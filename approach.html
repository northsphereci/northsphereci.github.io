Our Approach

A rigorous four-phase methodology

Every engagement follows a structured, adaptive process built on evidence, collaboration, and continuous learning.

1

Diagnosis

Deep context analysis, stakeholder mapping, systems thinking, and root cause assessment.

2

Co-Design

Collaborative strategy and solution design with clients and partners, grounded in evidence.

3

Implementation

Technically rigorous support through deployment, with adaptive management and course corrections.

4

Learning & Impact

Systematic evaluation, knowledge capture, and impact documentation to demonstrate value.

1
Phase 01

Diagnosis & Context Analysis

We begin by deeply understanding the problem landscape — its history, stakeholders, political economy, and root causes.

Stakeholder mapping and political economy analysis
Systems mapping and root cause assessment
Baseline data collection and evidence synthesis
Risk and opportunity identification
2
Phase 02

Co-Design & Strategy

Together with clients and key stakeholders, we design strategies and solutions that are evidence-based, contextually grounded, and politically feasible.

Theory of change co-development
Options analysis and scenario planning
Collaborative strategy workshops
Implementation planning and resource mapping
3
Phase 03

Implementation Support

We provide technically rigorous support throughout delivery, staying close to the work and ensuring adaptive management in practice.

Technical assistance and expert deployment
Adaptive management and real-time learning
Stakeholder facilitation and consensus building
Quality assurance and risk management
4
Phase 04

Learning & Impact Evaluation

We systematically document results, capture learning, and support clients in demonstrating the value and sustainability of change.

Mixed-methods evaluation design and implementation
Impact documentation and reporting
Knowledge management and lessons learned
Sustainability planning and exit strategy
